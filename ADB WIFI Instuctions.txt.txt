Explain stuff: 
-It's all Windows cmd stuff, even though you're in Android Studio
-Right click .bat files, click Edit
2020-2021 UBett\connect.bat -->
"echo" just puts text into terminal
"cd" is change directory (folder): move the command line to where the actual ADB is

AppData\Local\Android\Sdk\platform-tools\connect.bat -->
"pause" just pauses program to do stuff (Like plug in robot).
"adb tcpip 5555" Tells the robot to 'listen' for downloads over wireless (tcpip) on port 5555
"adb connect 192.168.43.1" Tells Android Studio to connect to the robot's IP address 
     (Rev Hub is 192.168.43.1 when connected to its WiFi) to download programs

AppData\Local\Android\Sdk\platform-tools\cn.bat -->
Just uses same 'adb connect' command. Shorthand for if Android Studio/laptop disconnects from robot,
and immediately reconnects to robot
